Lettering!!!

You have learned the basic building blocks and principles of calligraphy. These simple transitions, stroke, etc are the key to calligrapy. Just using this simple things, you can letter in calligrapy. Well, now is the time you can start! You can literally write a letter in calligrapy with the skills you learned.

Lettering With The Basic Strokes

You can letter using the strokes you just learned. You can connect different ones together to create your letter. For example, in h, you use the loop-down and upwards to downwards stroke to create the letter. In another example using the letter n, you use downstroke, and the up to down stroke. The pictures below show different letters using the 7 strokes.

To Keep In Mind

Alongside using these small skills to letter, keep in mind also when you are going down and when you are going up. No matter what you're lettering, always keep in mind to go thick when down and thin when up. This is important to remember so you dont always find yourself relying on the strokes but also have it fixed when to use different sides of the pen.
