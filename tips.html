Tip #1

When writing the letters in calligrapy, lift your pen up each letter. This way you will be able to get the best result.

Tip #2

Slow down! Usually in videos you might see the calligrapher writing quick, but that is usually because the video is speeded up. To get the best results go slowly so you dont make a mistake with your up/down strokes.

Tip #3

Put pressure on the downstroke. To get the most controlled, best, and steady result, put pressure on your pen as you do the downstroke. That way you will be ablt to differentiate your up/down strokes better.

Tip #4

Notice where you are drawing the thicker lines. Remember that the downstrokes and thicker lines go when you pen goes down. When you go up, it is thinner and it is an upstroke.
